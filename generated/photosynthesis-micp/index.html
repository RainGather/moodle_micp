How plants make their own food

Photosynthesis is the process by which plants convert light energy into chemical energy. Through a series of steps, plants take in carbon dioxide and water, use sunlight as fuel, and produce glucose and oxygen as outputs.

Process overview
CO₂ + H₂O + Light
↓
C₆H₁₂O₆ + O₂

Plants use chlorophyll — the green pigment in their leaves — to capture light energy and drive this chemical transformation.
